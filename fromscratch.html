California

Indian Wells, California

As a tennis player, Indian Wells is a dream come true. Watching world-class tennis at the BNP Paribas Open, soaking up the desert sun, and spending quality time with friends made this one of my favorite trips ever.

  1. BNP Paribas Open Tennis
    • Watching top-ranked players live
    • The electric stadium atmosphere
  2. Desert sunshine & heat
  3. Quality time with friends
    • We hung out at the pool and hottub most nights
    • Enjoyed the local cuisine
  4. Exploring nearby Palm Springs

  1. The Pacific Ocean
    • Exploring the tide pools
    • Watching the sunset over the water
  2. Main Beach & the boardwalk
    • We ate delcious Poke and fish tacos
    • Shopped in fun stores
  3. Wandering the pretty town
  4. Art galleries & local shops

My Top Travel Tips

01

Pack Light

Bring half of what you think you need. You can always buy things locally, and a light bag means less stress.

02

Learn Basic Phrases

Even a few words in the local language — hello, thank you, please — go a long way in building goodwill.

03

Book Accommodation Early

Especially for peak seasons. The best spots fill up fast, and prices spike last-minute.
