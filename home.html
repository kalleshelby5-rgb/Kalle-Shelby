About Me - Kalle Shelby

Kalle Shelby

Pre-Business · BYU Class of 2029 · Provo, UT

Hi, I'm Kalle! I'm a Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University with a minor in Psychology, originally from Spokane, Washington. I'm passionate about finance, people, and making the most of every opportunity life throws my way.

Whether I'm competing on the tennis court, exploring a new city, or spending time with my family and friends, I bring the same energy and commitment to everything I do. I grew up captaining my high school tennis and volleyball teams, built my own tennis coaching business, and spent summers working at a flower farm and wedding venue — experiences that shaped my work ethic and love for connecting with people.

At BYU I'm involved in the Women in Finance Association, Finance Society, Private Banking & Wealth Management club, and the Sport Psychology Club. I'm actively working toward a career in finance or business consulting and love every step of the journey.
